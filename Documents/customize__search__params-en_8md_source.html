1 Customize of search algorithm
2 =============================
3 
4 If you use accessors or mutators of [SearchParams](@ref Sayuri::SearchParams),
5 you can change Sayuri's search algorithm.
6 
7 By default, it's set up by my groundless intuition.
8 
9 The following constant values are indexes of arrays.
10 
11 * [NUM_PIECE_TYPES](@ref Sayuri::NUM_PIECE_TYPES): A number of piece types.
12  * [EMPTY](@ref Sayuri::EMPTY): It's not any piece.
13  * [PAWN](@ref Sayuri::PAWN): Pawn.
14  * [KNIGHT](@ref Sayuri::KNIGHT): Knight.
15  * [BISHOP](@ref Sayuri::BISHOP): Bishop.
16  * [ROOK](@ref Sayuri::ROOK): Rook.
17  * [QUEEN](@ref Sayuri::QUEEN): Queen.
18  * [KING](@ref Sayuri::KING): King.
19 * [NUM_SQUARES](@ref Sayuri::NUM_SQUARES): A number of squares on the board.
20  * [A1](@ref Sayuri::A1): A1 square.
21  * [B1](@ref Sayuri::B1): B1 square.
22  * [C1](@ref Sayuri::C1): C1 square.
23  * (Omitted from D1 to E8)
24  * [F8](@ref Sayuri::F8): F8 square.
25  * [G8](@ref Sayuri::G8): G8 square.
26  * [H8](@ref Sayuri::H8): H8 square.
