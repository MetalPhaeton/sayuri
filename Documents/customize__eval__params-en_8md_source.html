1 Customize of evaluation function
2 ================================
3 
4 If you use accessors or mutators of [EvalParams](@ref Sayuri::EvalParams),
5 you can change Sayuri's evaluation function.
6 
7 By default, It's set up by my experience of chess.
8 
9 Each evaluation score is calculated by &quot;(The weight) * (The value)&quot;.
10 And the score of the current node is the total of these scores.
11 
12 The following constant values are indexes of arrays.
13 
14 * [NUM_PIECE_TYPES](@ref Sayuri::NUM_PIECE_TYPES): A number of piece types.
15  * [EMPTY](@ref Sayuri::EMPTY): It's not any piece.
16  * [PAWN](@ref Sayuri::PAWN): Pawn.
17  * [KNIGHT](@ref Sayuri::KNIGHT): Knight.
18  * [BISHOP](@ref Sayuri::BISHOP): Bishop.
19  * [ROOK](@ref Sayuri::ROOK): Rook.
20  * [QUEEN](@ref Sayuri::QUEEN): Queen.
21  * [KING](@ref Sayuri::KING): King.
22 * [NUM_SQUARES](@ref Sayuri::NUM_SQUARES): A number of squares on the board.
23  * [A1](@ref Sayuri::A1): A1 square.
24  * [B1](@ref Sayuri::B1): B1 square.
25  * [C1](@ref Sayuri::C1): C1 square.
26  * (Omitted from D1 to E8)
27  * [F8](@ref Sayuri::F8): F8 square.
28  * [G8](@ref Sayuri::G8): G8 square.
29  * [H8](@ref Sayuri::H8): H8 square.

73 Weight Object
74 -------------
75 
76 [Weight](@ref Sayuri::Weight) object is an object to generate evaluation score
77 by multiplying the weight and the value.
78 
79 This object has 2 parameters, which are an opening(32 pieces exists) weight
80 and an ending(2 Kings only) weight.
81 
82 Actual weight is calculated by linear function of number of pieces on the
83 board with above parameters.
84 
85 * [opening_weight()](@ref Sayuri::Weight::opening_weight)
86  * A weight when opening(32 pieces exists).
87 
88 * [ending_weight()](@ref Sayuri::Weight::ending_weight)
89  * A weight when ending(2 Kings only).
